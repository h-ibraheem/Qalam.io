Our Story

Qalam Al Maarifa for Research and Development was founded in February 2025 in Riyadh as graduates of the Prince Misk Foundation- Launchpad 6.0, Qalam Al Maarifa emerged with a clear vision: to catalyze the development of the scientific research ecosystem in Saudi Arabia by transforming innovative ideas into tangible and impactful solutions. Our pioneering efforts address critical gaps in women’s health research while empowering institutions through smart, sustainable solutions.

Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions

What is the "Be Generous" Initiative and how does it work?

The "Be Generous" Initiative is our flagship program providing innovative menstrual health support through interactive awareness campaigns, community participation, and practical resources like hygiene accessories. We empower Saudi women with comprehensive health education aligned with Vision 2030.

How does your research address the 37% health information gap?

Our evidence-based women's health research specifically targets the critical gap where only 37% of young Saudi women receive adequate health information from their mothers. We provide comprehensive, culturally-sensitive health education and research solutions.

What makes your gamification training different from traditional health education?

Our gamification training uses interactive game-based learning, preparatory health games, and modern digital methodologies to make health education engaging and effective. This innovative approach increases retention and community participation in wellness programs.

How many research dialogue sessions do you conduct and what topics do you cover?

We've conducted 22+ monthly research dialogue sessions covering diverse scientific research topics, with special focus on women's health, Sudanese society health impacts, and community wellness. These sessions facilitate expert consultations and knowledge sharing.

What partnerships do you have and how do they benefit your clients?

We maintain strategic partnerships with WASH United for menstrual hygiene awareness and align with UN Sustainable Development Goals. Our collaboration with Prince Misk Foundation alumni network ensures high-quality, internationally-recognized research standards.

How do your services align with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030?

All our services directly support Vision 2030 by empowering Saudi women through health education, fostering innovation in research methodologies, building vibrant communities, and creating sustainable health solutions that improve quality of life for Saudi society.
